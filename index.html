The Eagle story

An eagle can live up to 70 years, has an accurate vision, they can focus on something as far as 5 km away. No matter what the obstacles, the eagle will not move his focus from the prey until he grabs it.

Meaning: no matter the circumstance you might find yourself in, no matter pain, no matter the sufferance, don`t give up on your dreams, don`t lose hope even though things don`t seem to go your way.

When the storms come, the eagle uses the storm wind to lift itself higher. Once above the storming clouds the eagle rests its wings, in the meantime, all the other birds hide in the branches and leaves of the tree. That is why the eagle does not fly with sparrows, ravens and other small birds, it flies alone and at high altitude.

Meaning: Don`t be afraid to face your deepest fears, by you facing your problems you will surely find a solution and overcome them, remember "What does not kill you, makes you stronger". And Don't associate yourself with those you think small, those who don't have a vision in life.

But to reach this age of 70, the eagle must make a life time decision. In its 40th year its long and flexible talons can no longer grab prey. Its long and sharp beak becomes bent, the feathers become old, thick and heavy feathers stick to its chest and makes it difficult to fly. Then the eagle is left with only two options: Die or go through a painful process of change.

The process requires that eagle fly to a mountain top and sit on its nest. Then the eagle knocks its beak against a rock until it plucks it out. Then the eagle will wait for a new beak to grow back and then it will pluck out its talons. When the its new talons grow back, the eagle starts plucking its old-aged feathers.
